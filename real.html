About Me

I am Divit Singhal from Hai Sing Catholic School. Welcome to my Digital portfolio! Learning Journey

The journey leading up to this point and what I have learnt

April 2026

VEX Based Crane

This year I made a VEX crane that can lift Heavy things. I made this with my friends because I wanted to learn how to independently build using VEX. After building the crane, I have learnt how to test for rounded screws, design robots using OnShape and building bases.

March 2026

Hackclub Campfire

I joined this compeition because it was a gamejam. I remember really wanting to join a gamejam, so I did earlier this year along with my classmate Dexden. The theme was beneath the surface and I decided to make a game titled "Grave Jumper". All made in pygame. This shows my Proficiency in Python. I know Object Oriented Programming and Data structures and algorithms. From this game jam, I learnt time management(there was a massive time crunch and i cut so much content) and Teamwork. If there was another opportunity i would definitely do it again.

July 2025

VJC 2025

Prior to the actual competition, I had to design my robot and build it. I thought a of a robot idea and proceeded with it. My teammates were not very cooperative so i had to deal with them somehow. just kept them busy with getting me the parts i needed or telling them to build parts of the robot so they don't slack. If they did, i would just shout at them so that they work. From this experience I learnt how to be a better leader

May 2025

RoboClash 2025

This competition was a new experience for me. I have never participated in a competition where we were using proper electronics and 3d printed robots. This entire competition was made of flimsy 3d printed parts and an ESP32. I remember that the wires were disconnected from the ESP32 and i had to solder it back on. eventually we got 4th place, we would have placed better but my junior touched my robot 5 minutes before the competition there was some technical error and i could not fix it in time. So we got 4th place. I learnt to be more adaptable and patient after this competition

April 2025

Flappy Bird

I Made this after learning Object Oriented Programming in C++. I had to download SFML and download assets from the web to make this game. I thought downloading SFML would be a simple task. However, it proved to be a real challenge. I had to search multiple tutorials to just get it working. I learnt to be resilient and got better at C++, I have used this C++ knowledge in my 2025 VJC and Crane project.

Game Development

During the december holidays i decided to try game Development. After Playing Halo reach, the game pulled my heartstrings and I wanted to make something similar to it so i got into gamedev. There was this company called Bungie that made this, They were really determined to make a fun game for everybody to enjoy, i thought of doing the same so i decided to start learning unreal engine( a game engine) and i really enjoyed it and have been doing it ever since.

Music Production

I used Garage band and FL studio to make my beats. I got inspired after seeing this producer, Nick Mira, making beats within minutes and sounding majestic. I thought I could do something similar. At first it sounded horrid but after some practise and learning how to mix different sounds and hpw to use the Equaliser, I eventually got better at it and have been producing ever since.
