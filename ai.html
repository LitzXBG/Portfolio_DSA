About Me

I am Divit Singhal from Hai Sing Catholic School. Welcome to my Digital portfolio! This is where I have showcased all of my talents and achievements all the way from the start of my journey to where I currently am. From my first solar powered car to my crane VEX based robot made this year. I am deeply interested in Robotics and coding. I have made many projects incorporating these aspects.

I have quite some experience in coding especially Python and C++ (including for VEX). I was also an organiser for this youth coding organisation called BuildingBLocs for about a year and I'll rejoin again after my O levels. I have learnt to be very open to new ideas and learnt how to manage large amounts of people.

I have all of these interests and you might perceive me as a nerd. However, I actually do enjoy volunteering, debating (participating in Mock Law Summits) and Tennis! I really hope you enjoy my digital space where I get to tell you more about myself!
